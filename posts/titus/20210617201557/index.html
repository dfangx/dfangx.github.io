Titus 2

Posted at — Jun 17, 2021

In contrast to the people described, Titus is instructed to teach what is in agreement with sound doctrine. He is to teach others in both instruction and example. People should live in a way such that it does not bring ridicule to the word of God. The doctrine that is believed in must be reflected in how they live. v.1-10

They are to teach and do all these things because of the appearance of the grace of God. This grace trains them to live righteously while waiting for the second coming. Everything is to be done because of what Jesus did to redeem the people. All these things are to be declared with authority, not kept to themselves. v.11-15
