Day 49, Leviticus 16-18

Posted at — May 28, 2021

God gives laws for the Day of Atonement, a day which Aaron is to make atonement for the entirety of Israel. This is one of the only times Aaron is able to enter inside the veil. Atonement is made for the priest first and then the people. Incense must be burned on the altar inside the veil so that the cloud of incense covers Aaron before God. Another goat is used as a scapegoat for Israel’s sins, which is sent to die. God makes it very clear that he is holy. Furthermore, God emphasizes that the only place of sacrifice is where he is. God reveals that the life of a living creature is in the blood. In doing so, this also tells us that atonement is made by the sacrifice of life. Anyone who eats blood is cut off from the people. Having established methods to allow himself to dwell with his people, God gives laws regarding how Israel should live as a distinct people. God warns the people that just as the land vomited out the Canaanites because of their iniquities, the same thing will happen to the Israelites if they do not keep his statutes.
