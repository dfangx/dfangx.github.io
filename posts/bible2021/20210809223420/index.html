Day 122, Psalm 133

Posted at — Aug 9, 2021

Psalm 133 expresses how good it is when there is unity among brothers. It compares it to the anointing oil running down the beard of Aaron the priest and the blessing of rain that falls on the city of Zion. The author makes it clear that the unity of brothers is such because of the presence and blessings of God. Thus, the author associates the anointment of the priest and the blessings of God with the unity of brothers, present because God is present among them.
