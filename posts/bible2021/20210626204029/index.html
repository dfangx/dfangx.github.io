Day 78, Deuteronomy 24-27

Posted at — Jun 26, 2021

Laws for divorce are given to Israel, in addition to various other laws. Some laws include reminders of past experiences that Israel had with whatever the law is addressing. For example, the law concerning leprosy was associated with the case when Miriam got leprosy. The law also demonstrates God’s love to sojourners, as the Israelites are instructed to leave the parts of their harvest they forget about. God also gives laws concerning what happens when the husband dies in a marriage. This immediately brings to mind the instance regarding Judah and Tamar back in Genesis. God also singles out Amalek for their treatment of Israel, and Israel is to completely destroy Amalek. We see God’s providence of Israel in the following law, for it communicates to us that God is the one who provided for not only Israel’s entry into Canaan but also their life in Canaan. The Israelites are to remember where they came from and what God has done for them. Regarding the curses to be spoken on Mount Ebal, the last one stands out, as it not only summarizes what God is requesting of Israel, but it sets a very high standard on how to live.
