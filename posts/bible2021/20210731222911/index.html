Day 113, 1 Chronicles 1-2

Posted at — Jul 31, 2021

1 Chronicles opens up with a genealogy from Adam to David. This makes sense as the book of Chronicles was written while Israel was in exile. I don’t actually understand why all these names are included, as there are names that I don’t recognize from previous books. However, the purpose of this genealogy appears to be so that Israel is reminded of the lineage through which she came from. The listing of all these names indicate that there isn’t anything special about them - they are one of many nations that Abraham fathered. Yet, out of all these nations, God chose them to be his people. I do recognize most of the names from verses 1-26, as they can be cross referenced to Genesis 5. However, the latter half of chapter 1 and most of chapter 2 are unfamiliar and extremely confusing.
