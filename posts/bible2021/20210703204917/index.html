Day 85, Joshua 12-15

Posted at — Jul 3, 2021

Moses defeated the kings east of the Jordan. The land there was given to the tribes of Reuben, Gad, and the half-tribe of Manasseh. Of the kings west of the Jordan, Joshua defeated thirty-one of them. Even after all that, there was still land to be conquered. This shows the large scale of the task set before Joshua. Of the nations left over, God tells Joshua that he will drive them out before Israel. Of the tribes of Israel, Levi had no portion among the Israelites; they were given cities to dwell in, with God as their inheritance. Caleb was also allotted land for his obedience to God during the incident with the twelve spies. His inheritance was among Judah. Among the cities given to Judah was Jerusalem - however, they were unable to drive out the Jebusites who lived there.
