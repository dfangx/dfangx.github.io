Day 95, Judges 16-18

Posted at — Jul 13, 2021

The story of Samson continues with Samson falling for a prostitute and almost getting ambushed. Afterwards, he falls for Delilah. The Philistine lords tasked her with finding out Samson’s weakness. After several failed attempts at persuasion, Samson finally falls for the Philistines' trap amd gets himself captured and humiliated. On a day of celebration for the Philistines, Samson was brought out to entertain the lords. All the lords present at the celebration were killed by Samson when he caused the building to collapse. The next story demonstrates how easy it is to mix up one’s relationship with God with the surrounding culture. Micah and the Levite most likely thought they were worshipping God by creating the statue. However, this not only led them to sin, but also the entire tribe of Dan to sin. Furthermore, Dan fought the people of Laish, whom the author emphasized as secure and lacking nothing. The demonstration of Israel’s corruption is further emphasized when Jonathan, a descendant of Moses, was appointed priest for the metal image.
