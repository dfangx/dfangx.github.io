Day 21, Genesis 27-29

Posted at — Apr 30, 2021

As the firstborn, Esau is called in to Isaac so that he may eat of the game Esau hunts and bless him. The fruits of favouritism are born here, when Rebekah tells Jacob to trick Isaac so he could get the blessing. The scheme is successful, and Jacob takes the blessing from Esau. Esau shows his lack of respect for his parents when he vows to kill Jacob for stealing his blessing. Rebekah hearing this, asks Isaac to prevent Jacob from taking a wife from the Hittite women. Isaac thus sends Jacob to Laban. Esau, on hearing that his wives displeased his parents, takes on more wives from his relative Ishmael to try to please them. God reveals himself to Jacob in a dream and reveals that Jacob is the inheritor of the Abrahamic covenant. On waking up, Jacob vows that God will be his God should he return to his father’s home, and he will give to God a tenth of all he has should it be so. Jacob eventually reaches his uncle Laban. He in turn is deceived by Laban and as a result, ends up working for Laban 14 years for Rachel and Lea. Lea gives birth to Simeon, Levi, and Judah.
