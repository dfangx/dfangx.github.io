Day 77, Deuteronomy 21-23

Posted at — Jun 26, 2021

Many of the laws given here have recurring themes. Their punishments are harsh so that whatever evil that had been committed in Israel can be purged. Again, these punishments are often delivered for intentional transgressions. In addition, many of the judgements that are rendered are done with the involvement of the congregation. There are also laws regarding sexual immorality, which are also to be addressed in the presence of witnesses. God lays out the laws concerning who may or may not be included in the assembly of God. This includes the exclusion of the Ammonites and Moabites. The children of the Edomites and Egyptians are allowed beginning in the third generation. Many of these laws demonstrate God’s holiness and are based on his holiness. Who God is dictates every aspect of the life and interactions of an Israelite. Although these laws may be a little hard to understand, they demonstrates how tightly intertwined one’s relationship with God is with how they live their life.
