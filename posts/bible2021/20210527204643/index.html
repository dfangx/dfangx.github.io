Day 48, Leviticus 14-15

Posted at — May 27, 2021

God gives laws regarding bodily discharges and the cleansing of lepers and houses. Throughout the entire passage, God makes it very clear that there is a tight relationship between one’s physical state and spiritual state. When a leper is healed, a sacrifice is associated with the healing. Similar to today, they are also to wash themselves after recovering from the disease. Likewise, the cleansing of the house of a leper is also associated with the blood of a sacrifice and physical cleansing with water. With bodily discharges, whether it be semen or blood, there is an association with physical cleansing. Furthermore, atonement is needed if a woman discharges blood apart from her menstrual period. The constant association between sacrifice, atonement, physical cleansing, disease, and other physical states, give a constant reminder that one’s spiritual and physical lives are not separate but rather intricately related. By giving these laws, God also shows that he cares for his people’s physical well-being, while reminding his people that many of these physical ailments or conditions have something to do with man’s sinful condition.
