Day 38, Exodus 25-27

Posted at — May 17, 2021

God gives instructions for the sanctuary so that he can dwell among the people. The materials to be collected are to be given by those whose hearts have been moved. The Ark of the Covenant is made first. It is a rectangularly shaped object made of wood and overlaid with gold. On the ark sits the mercy seat, with the wings of two cherubim overshadowing it. In it lies the testimony from God. The mercy seat is where God will meet with Moses. The bread of the Presence is to be set on a table made with wood and overlaid with gold. In addition, a golden lampstand with seven lamps is to be made. Following this, instructions for the tabernacle are given. It is made of curtains, with cherubim woven into them. A veil is also to be made in a similar manner, which will separate the Holy Place from the Most Holy. The ark is to be set inside the Most Holy Place, with the lampstand and table just outside the curtain. Further out lie the court of the tabernacle along with a bronze altar. A charge is given to Aaron and his descendants to always keep the lamp burning.
