Day 129, 2 Samuel 8-9, 1 Chronicles 18

Posted at — Aug 16, 2021

God gave David victory over his enemies wherever he went. He defeated the Philistines, Moab, Edom, Syria, and more. The fame of David spread far, and because of these victories, other nations brought gifts to David. These nations bore witness to David’s dedication of all the silver and gold to God. In addition, David desired to show kindness to the house of Saul for the sake of Jonathan. He took Mephibosheth the son of Jonathan into his care, restoring to him his land. Perhaps the reign of David was a glimpse of what God had intended back in the garden of Eden, when God instructed man to rule over creation. David ruled over his people expressing who God is: with justice and equity.
