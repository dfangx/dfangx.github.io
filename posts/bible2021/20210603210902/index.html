Day 55, Numbers 3-4

Posted at — Jun 3, 2021

A count of the sons of Aaron and the Levites is performed. At the same time, the duties of the Levites are given. The sons of Gershon were put in charge of guarding and servicing the hangings and coverings of the tabernacle, the Kohathites were in charge of guarding and servicing the items in the tabernacle, and the sons of Merari were put in charge of guarding and servicing the frames of the tabernacle. God also commands Aaron and his sons to always wrap up the holy things for the Kohathites to carry whenever the Israelites moved. In addition, instead of the firstborn of Israel,the Levites were dedicated to God. Because there were more Israelite firstborn than Levites, the remaining firstborn were redeemed by a payment. This payment went to Aaron and his sons. Moses and Aaron obeyed God’s commandments. Thus, for the people chosen to minister before God, God provided a way for it to happen without immediate death for them.
