Day 43, Exodus 39-40

Posted at — May 22, 2021

The Israelites made the priestly garments as God commanded Moses. They made the ephod, the engraved onyx stones, the breastpiece with its four rows of jewels, the blue robe of the ephod, the bells and pomegranates, coats, turban, sash, and the inscribed plate of the crown. They did as God had commanded, and Moses blessed them. God gave the command for the tabernacle to be erected on the first day of the first month. The ark is to be placed in the tabernacle, with the veil screening the ark. Before the veil is the table, the lampstand, and the altar for incense. The door to the tabernacle is to be screened. Outside the door is the altar of burnt offering, with the basin of water placed between the tent and the altar. The outer court surrounds all of that, with its gate screened. Everything in the tabernacle, along with Aaron and his sons is to be consecrated with the anointing oil. The garments are to be put on Aaron. Moses obeyed all of this. Thus, God dwelled among his people within the tabernacle in the cloud of glory, and the people moved and rested according to whatever God did.
