Day 117, 1 Chronicles 6

Posted at — Aug 4, 2021

The author recounts the genealogy of Levi. Of the sons of Kohath came Aaron, Moses, and Ahitub. The line of Kohath was recorded until the exile by Babylon. The sons of Levi also served in the tabernacle of God, ministering through song. However, the sons of Aaron ministered by making the offerings on the altar of burnt offering and incense. They were in charge of the Most Holy Place and also made atonement for Israel. The Levites did not have their own inheritance; rather, they were given cities and pastures within the borders of their brothers. The sons of Aaron received cities in Judah. The rest of the Kohathites received land within Manasseh and Emphraim. The Gershonites received land from Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and Manasseh. The Merarites were given land from Reuben, Gad, and Zebulun.
