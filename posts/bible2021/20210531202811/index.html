Day 52, Leviticus 24-25

Posted at — May 31, 2021

Commands regarding the arrangements for the lamp and bread of the tabernacle are given. The punishment for blasphemy is death, though this law is different in that it calls for the entire congregation of Israel to stone the blasphemer. Such is the severity of blaspheming the name of God - it does not concern one or two people, but rather the entire congregation of the people of God. Furthermore, any bodily injury that is caused to another is to be repaid equally. Commands regarding the Sabbath year and year of Jubilee is given. Just as the Sabbath day is for the people to rest, the Sabbath year is for the land to rest. The year of Jubilee comes every 50 years, and is a day when certain transactions concerning an Israelite or their property may be reversed. An Israelite is able to redeem his own property or his freedom if he sold it previously. If he does not have the means to do it, then someone else may redeem him. The Israelites are to show kindness to one another and to redeem each other should the situation call for it.
