Day 1, Genesis 1-3

Posted at — Apr 10, 2021

The world before creation is described as formless, empty, and dark. Yet, the presence of God is there, it is clear that wherever God is, something is bound to happen. God exercises his power by simply speaking things into creation. He first forms the earth and then fills it. Then, he creates man in his image - to rule under God, to work for God, and to live with God. There was one rule - to not eat of the tree of knowledge of good and evil. Eating of it would bring death. But the woman was tempted by the serpent, and although created in God’s image, she saw the fruit of knowledge of good and evil as something desirable. The woman listened to the tempter, ate the fruit, and gave it to her husband. The result was a series of curses and expulsion from the presence of God in the garden.
