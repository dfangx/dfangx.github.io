Day 80, Deuteronomy 30-31

Posted at — Jun 28, 2021

Moses tells Israel that after the blessing and the curse have come to pass, then Israel will remember God, and God will bring them back from exile. He will make Israel even more prosperous than before in all things. God will take delight in them when they turn to God with their entire being. Israel now has two choices. One choice is good and life, should Israel walk in line with the commandments God has given. The other is evil and death, which is when Israel turns away from God. Moses urges them to choose life, and obey God in all that they do. Joshua is then commissioned to lead Israel into Canaan. Moses assures his successor that God will go before Israel and drive out the peoples of the land for them. Moses also commands that the law be read out loud every seven years wherever God chooses to assemble the people. God tells Moses that despite everything that has been done, Israel will fall away. Thus, he tells Moses to write a song, which will be a witness against them when they sin.
