About

Posted at — Apr 25, 2021

Hi there! Nice to meet you! I go by the name of dfangx (obviously that isn’t my real name), but anyhow…

I graduated from Queen’s University in Kingston, Canada in 2021. Now I am a full time software developer at IBM. Some of my hobbies include coding, studying the Bible, playing Total War games, watching anime, and playing the piano.

Throughout my academic career I’ve been able to work on many fun projects, which you can find on my Github . I’ve recently taken an interest in the Wayland protocol, and as a Linux user, finding different ways to tinker with/break my computer.

You’ll find on this website a collection of different notes and write ups mainly on technology and Bible readings, though I may occasionally diverge from that. This site is very much a work in progress, so don’t expect too much! I just hope that this can become a useful resource for people interested in technology, the Bible, and the other random things I decide to write about
